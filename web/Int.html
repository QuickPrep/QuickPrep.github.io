How to Introduce Yourself ?

How to Introduce yourself is the Million Dollor question where people always tend to search the solution for.
If you are one among them,then you are at the right place.
Below are 4 rules to follow to hit this Question.
All the Best !

  • Good Quality

    • Start your Introduction saying your good quality and why do u believe that is a good Quality !

  • Aspirations

    • Talk About your Aspiration!Where you want to see yourself in next years

  • Inspirations

    • Take Your Inspirations Out,What made you to get into this particular field of study

  • Technical Skills

    • Speak about your skills that matches the Company's Objective or Requirements

    Hello Sir/Madam,

    I am John Doe , i would like to tell one good quality i possess ,that i have pretty good patience.The reason why i believe that's a good quality is because,It is important to be patient and keep going to reach our goals.

    I would like to become a leading Programmer who solves real world problems. Any Programmer can write software but the one who writes efficiently would stand out of the crowd is what i believe and i wish to be part of it.

    The reason why i got into this field of study is that i have been watching the Technology that's evolving , and i used to be very much curious on how those programmatically works and how they have made lives easy ; which actually inspired me to get into this .

    Ultimately i have been learning programming languages since 2 years such as C Java and PHP and Front end technologies like HTML,CSS,Javascript. And it would be great if i become a part of this organization to utilize my skillset for the growth of the organization.
